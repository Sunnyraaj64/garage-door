Common Problems with Garage Door Panels

  • Dents and Dings: One of the most frequent issues is cosmetic damage. Dents can occur due to accidents or even minor impacts, which can detract from your garage's appeal. Although this may seem like a minor issue, it can lead to more significant problems if not addressed promptly.

  • Cracks and Breaks: Over time, exposure to extreme weather conditions can weaken garage door panels, leading to cracks or breaks. These issues not only affect the door's appearance but can also pose safety risks.

  • Warping: Wooden panels are particularly susceptible to warping due to moisture and temperature changes. This can result in an uneven door that doesn't operate smoothly, leading to further mechanical issues.

  • Rust and Corrosion: Metal panels may develop rust, especially in humid environments. Rust not only impacts the aesthetics of your garage door but can also weaken its structure over time.

  • Misalignment: Panels can become misaligned due to improper installation or damage. This misalignment can cause the door to stick or become inoperable, creating a frustrating experience for homeowners.
